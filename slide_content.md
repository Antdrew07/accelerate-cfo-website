## Slide 3: Logo System — Four Flexible Components
**The Accelerate CFO Logo System**

The logo system consists of four primary components that work together across all touchpoints:

1. **Primary Wordmark** — Full "ACCELERATE CFO" in elegant uppercase serif. "ACCELERATE" in cream/ivory, "CFO" in 24K gold. Integrated upward arrow motif in the "A" symbolizes growth and acceleration.

2. **Icon Mark** — Stylized uppercase "A" with upward-pointing arrow in 24K gold. Used for favicons, app icons, social media profiles, and watermarks.

3. **Horizontal Lockup** — Icon mark + vertical gold separator + wordmark. Preferred for social media covers, presentation title slides, and wide-format applications.

4. **Social Profile Mark** — Circular composition with icon mark centered within a gold ring. Designed for LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, and X profile pictures.

## Slide 6: Typography System — Editorial Tension Between Authority and Accessibility
**Typography Pairing**

**Primary: EB Garamond** — Display and headline typeface. Classical proportions and refined letterforms evoke the gravitas of financial institutions and editorial publications. Used for all headlines, section titles, pull quotes, and the logo wordmark.

Weights: Regular (400) for pull quotes | Medium (500) for subheadings | SemiBold (600) for section headers | Bold (700) for hero headlines

**Secondary: Source Sans 3** — Body and UI typeface. Open letterforms and generous x-height ensure readability at all sizes, from navigation labels to paragraph text.

Weights: Light (300) for captions | Regular (400) for body | SemiBold (600) for navigation | Bold (700) for UI emphasis

**Key Rules:** Eyebrow labels use uppercase with 0.15em letter-spacing in Source Sans 3 SemiBold. Headlines in EB Garamond use tight negative letter-spacing (-0.02em). Body text maintains minimum 1.6 line-height.

## Slide 7: Type Scale — Precision Hierarchy for Every Element
**Complete Type Scale**

| Element | Font | Size | Weight | Spacing |
|---------|------|------|--------|---------|
| Hero Headline | EB Garamond | 64–80px | Bold 700 | -0.02em |
| Section Title | EB Garamond | 40–48px | SemiBold 600 | -0.01em |
| Card Title | EB Garamond | 24–28px | Medium 500 | 0 |
| Eyebrow Label | Source Sans 3 | 12–14px | SemiBold 600 | 0.15em |
| Body Text | Source Sans 3 | 16–18px | Regular 400 | 0.01em |
| Caption | Source Sans 3 | 13–14px | Light 300 | 0.02em |
| Button | Source Sans 3 | 14–16px | SemiBold 600 | 0.1em |

Every typographic decision reinforces the editorial luxury aesthetic — tight headlines for density and visual weight, wide-spaced labels for the editorial "label" effect, and generous body text for readability.

## Slide 8: Logo Usage Rules — Protecting Brand Integrity
**Logo Usage Guidelines**

**Clear Space:** Minimum clear space around any logo variant equals the height of the "C" in "CFO." No graphic elements, text, or imagery should encroach on this space.

**Minimum Sizes:**
| Variant | Print | Digital |
|---------|-------|---------|
| Full Wordmark | 1.5" wide | 150px wide |
| Icon Mark | 0.5" | 32px |
| Horizontal Lockup | 2" wide | 200px wide |

**Prohibited Uses:** Never stretch, rotate, recolor outside approved palette, place on busy backgrounds without dark overlay, add drop shadows or outer glows, or modify in any way. The "A" arrow motif must never be separated from the wordmark.

## Slide 9: Brand Voice — Authoritative Yet Approachable
**Voice & Tone Guidelines**

The Accelerate CFO voice is authoritative but approachable, precise but warm, confident but humble. It speaks with the expertise of a Fortune 500 CFO and the heart of a ministry partner.

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Website | Commanding, direct | "Your Church Deserves a CFO Who Understands the Mission." |
| Services | Expert, reassuring | "We ensure pastors receive every lawful tax benefit." |
| Social | Warm, educational | "73% of churches don't have a formal budget process." |
| Email | Professional, personal | "Barry and the team wanted to share insights..." |
| Proposals | Precise, value-driven | "Our assessment identifies an average of $47K in savings." |

**Use:** "Executive-level," "Kingdom-focused," "stewardship," "accelerate," "mission-driven"
**Avoid:** "Cheap," "discount," "basic," "simple," "budget-friendly"

## Slide 10: Photography & Brand Pattern — Cinematic and Inclusive
**Photography Style**

All photography should be cinematic, warm-toned, and race-neutral. Avoid images featuring specific individuals or racial groups to maintain inclusivity.

**Preferred Subjects:** Architecture (churches, modern buildings, skylines), landscapes (sunrises, horizons), abstract textures (gold, marble, leather), financial/business environments.

**Image Treatment:** Dark overlay (60–80% opacity) maintains text readability. Gradient runs from solid dark (#0A0F1C at 85%) on the left to semi-transparent on the right.

**Brand Pattern:** Signature interlocking gold chevrons and upward arrows on deep navy at 10–15% opacity. Used on business cards, presentation slides, social media graphics, and website section dividers.
